About the Pepeha Creator

The Pepeha Creator is a web application designed to help individuals create their own pepeha, a way of introducing oneself in Māori culture. A pepeha typically includes references to one's ancestral connections to landmarks, such as mountains, rivers, and tribal affiliations.

This tool allows users to input their details and generate a personalized pepeha that can be shared or printed. It is designed with simplicity and cultural sensitivity in mind.

This website is designed to cater to all users, both Māori and non-Māori, who wish to create and share their pepeha. We prioritize accessibility, ease of use, and cultural sensitivity, ensuring that users of all backgrounds can engage with the process of creating their pepeha. The website follows essential UX principles, including intuitive navigation, clear guidance, consistent design, and inclusive language, making it a user-friendly platform for everyone.

If you have any questions or feedback, feel free to reach out to us. We appreciate your input to make this platform better.
